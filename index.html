Isa Koyuki Lau

Isa Lau (she/her) is a NYC based dancer and choreographer. She currently studies dance at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts.
Isa was a part of Flight Path Dance Project's 6th Season, and has performed repertory works by Keerati Jinakunwiphat, Ashley Lindsey, Stephanie Martinez, Sekou McMiller, Yusha-Marie Sorzano, and Akira Uchida with the company.
She will continue her studies in dance at SUNY Purchase Conservatory of Dance.
